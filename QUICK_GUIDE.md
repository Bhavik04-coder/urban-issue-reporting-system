## 🗑️ Delete Your Reports

### Step-by-Step:

1. **Open "My Issues" Tab**
   - Tap the "My Issues" icon in bottom navigation (3rd icon)

2. **Find Your Report**
   - Scroll through your submitted reports
   - Each report shows title, status, and details

3. **Delete the Report**
   
   **Option A: Tap Delete Icon**
   - Tap the red trash icon (🗑️) on the right side of the report card
   
   **Option B: Long Press**
   - Long press anywhere on the report card

4. **Confirm Deletion**
   - A dialog will appear asking "Delete Report?"
   - Shows the report title
   - Tap "Delete" to confirm or "Cancel" to abort

5. **Wait for Confirmation**
   - Loading indicator shows "Deleting report..."
   - Success message appears: "Report deleted successfully"
   - Report disappears from your list

### Important Notes:
- ✅ You can only delete YOUR OWN reports
- ❌ You cannot delete reports created by others
- ⚠️ Deletion is PERMANENT and cannot be undone
- 🔒 Requires authentication (must be logged in)

## 🎨 Theme Toggle

### Switch Between Light & Dark Mode:

1. **Go to Profile**
   - Tap the "Profile" icon in bottom navigation (4th icon)

2. **Find Settings Section**
   - Scroll down to "Settings" section

3. **Toggle Theme**
   - Find the "Dark Mode" / "Light Mode" switch
   - Tap to toggle between themes
   - Theme changes instantly across entire app

4. **Theme Persists**
   - Your choice is saved automatically
   - App remembers your preference on next launch

### Theme Colors:
- **Saffron** (#FF9933) - Primary buttons and actions
- **Green** (#138808) - Success states and resolved issues
- **Blue** (#000080) - Info and links
- **White/Dark** - Backgrounds adapt to theme

## 📝 Report an Issue

### Quick Report with AI:

1. **Tap "Report" Tab**
   - 2nd icon in bottom navigation

2. **Take Photo**
   - Tap camera icon
   - Take photo of the issue
   - AI automatically detects issue type

3. **Confirm Location**
   - Tap GPS icon to auto-detect location
   - Or enter address manually

4. **Set Urgency**
   - Choose: Low, Medium, or High

5. **Submit**
   - Tap "Submit Report"
   - AI assigns to correct department
   - Track status in "My Issues"

### AI Features:
- 🤖 Auto-detects: Potholes, Garbage, Water Leakage, Streetlights
- 🏢 Auto-assigns to: Road, Sanitation, Water, or Electricity dept
- 📝 Auto-generates title and description
- 📍 GPS location capture

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### Can't Delete Report?
- ✅ Make sure you're logged in
- ✅ Check that it's YOUR report (not someone else's)
- ✅ Try refreshing the page
- ✅ Check internet connection

### Theme Not Changing?
- ✅ Make sure you're in Profile → Settings
- ✅ Tap the switch (not just the label)
- ✅ Hard refresh if on web (Ctrl+Shift+R)

### Report Not Submitting?
- ✅ Check all required fields are filled
- ✅ Ensure photo is uploaded
- ✅ Verify location is set
- ✅ Check internet connection
